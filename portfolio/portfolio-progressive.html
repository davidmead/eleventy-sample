Native app, Progressive Insurance (2013).

Acted as the User Experience Designer and Information Architect for the project.

Overview

At this point Progressive did not have a unified app strategy for mobile. The iOS version had limited functionality and information available. The Android version just redirected to a web page.

Goals

The existing app had a 1 star rating in both app stores. We needed to offer functional parity for both iOS & Android users.

After user interviews and competitive analysis we drew up a shortlist of minimum functionality:

  • All users having the ability to pay by credit card, EFT, and online check
  • Electronic ID card
  • Save or send a copy of Electronic ID from the app
  • Online Claims process
  • Find a Service Center
  • Agent details
  • Policy details

Work

  • Took the existing app and completely redesigned it based on user feedback and new business direction
  • Provided screen layouts, navigation architecture, and flows (payments, online claims, ID cards) in Axure
  • Worked closely with developers and visual designers for both iOS & Android
  • Acted as Systems Analyst for some of the internal processes
  • Designed a "customer-centric" model for the app over a "system-centric" model
  • Made sure we used as much of the native controls for each platform.

Results

After launch we saw an increase in new users as well as a continued rise in returning users, and completions of Claims within the app.

Also, after v3.0 and v3.1, we saw both Android & iOS store reviews go from a 1 star to a 4.5 star average rating (4.8 for iOS, 4.3 for Android).

This was the first project within Progressive using Axure to design/develop and host the mockups, wireframes, and requirements.
